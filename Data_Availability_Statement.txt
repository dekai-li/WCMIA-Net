The supporting data generated in this study have been deposited in Zenodo under a CC-BY-4.0 license and are publicly available at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.xxxxxxxx. The deposited data include the values underlying the quantitative results reported in the manuscript, the values used to generate figures, ablation and parameter sensitivity results, confusion matrices, training/test split information, and experimental configuration files.

The original datasets used in this study are publicly available third-party benchmark datasets and are not redistributed by the authors. The MUUFL Gulfport hyperspectral-LiDAR dataset is available at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.1186326. The Berlin-Urban-Gradient dataset is available at https://doi.org/10.2312/enmap.2016.002, with supplementary data available at https://doi.org/10.5880/enmap.2016.002. The Augsburg MDAS dataset is available at https://doi.org/10.14459/2022mp1657312. The Houston2018 dataset is available from the 2018 IEEE GRSS Data Fusion Contest / University of Houston data access webpage.

The source code will be released at https://github.com/dekai-li/WCMIA-Net upon acceptance and is also available from the corresponding author upon reasonable request.
